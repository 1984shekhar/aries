Components

ServiceMix distribution comes with several ready to use standard JBI components:

  • SM30UG:servicemix-http: an HTTP/SOAP Binding Component
  • SM30UG:servicemix-jms: an JMS/SOAP Binding Component
  • SM30UG:servicemix-jsr181: a Service Engine to deploy (annotated) POJOs as services
  • SM30UG:servicemix-eip: a Service Engine containing standard routing / flows patterns
  • SM30UG:servicemix-wsn2005: WS-Notification Service Engine
  • SM30UG:servicemix-bpe: a BPEL engine

Have a look at the Component Matrix for a comprehensive, detailed list of components and their properties.
